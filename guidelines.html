Return Policy

Items can be returned wi hin 10 days of delivery under the following conditions:

  • The product is damaged, defective, or significantly different from the description.
  • It must be unused and in its original packaging with all tags intact.
  • Returns must be initiated via our customer support portal or email.
  • Refunds are processed within 7 working days after product inspection.

Replacement Policy

Replacements are allowed for eligible items within 7 days of delivery. Conditions include:

  • Product must be damaged or incorrect as per the original order.
  • Replacement requests must include clear photos of the issue.
  • Pickup of the defective item will be arranged by us.
  • A new item will be shipped once the issue is verified by our team.

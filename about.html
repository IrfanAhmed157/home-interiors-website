Our Story

Maison Interiors was founded in 2021 by visionary designer Irfan Ahmed, driven by a passion for transforming everyday spaces into stunning, functional environments. What started as a personal journey into creative design has grown into a beloved brand known for modern elegance and refined taste.

With a background in interior architecture and a love for curated aesthetics, Irfan launched Maison Interiors to help people design homes that tell their own stories—beautifully and effortlessly.

Our Mission

At Maison Interiors, our mission is to empower individuals to design spaces that bring comfort, creativity, and joy. We believe that good design isn’t just about style—it’s about creating harmony and enhancing everyday living.

Our Services

  • Interior Designing Products: Bespoke spatial products tailored to your needs and style.
  • Furniture Products: Custom furniture creations that combine form and function.
  • Décor Products: A handpicked collection of stylish home accessories, art, and lighting.

Our Achievements

Since our inception, we’ve grown into a trusted name in home design. Some of our proud milestones include:

  • Featured in Elle Decor India and Architectural Digest.
  • Created over 150 unique interior design products.
  • Rated 4.9 stars by customers on major e-commerce platforms.
